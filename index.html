ABOUT US

We are one of China’s most professional private Inspection Companies.

We work in a wide range of Industries including Power Generation, Storage, Reclaimer & Stacker, Conveyor, Oil and Gas, Mining, Pressure equipment, Castings and fabrications, Ship-building & repair. etc.

We provide wide inspection services - Weld Inspection, Coating Inspection, NDT, Thickness Measurement, Hydrostatic Test, HSE Consultant. etc.

We are an Experience based Management Solution Provider.

We are an CNAS certified NDT company.

We are registered as a member of AINDT (Australia).

We are the recognized preferred sub-contractor of DNVGL, CCS.

We are an authorized vendor of AUMUND, SCHADE, Monadelphous, Sinostruct, Metso, McConnell Dowell , Pacific National, Austman, Tenova/Takraf, ARK, AGC Ausgroup, Oriental Electric. Etc. for Inspection and NDT.
